Script to fetch all users anime lists from myanimelist.com

You see, MAL has a shitty way of determining average score. There are people (or children) that put 10-s to everything. To deal with each person's individual score system, one must use not his absolute anime score, but the difference between this score and the average of all this person's scores.

I have few more idea-s and desire to research the stuff. For all these purposes i need the data from MAL. That's what this script is for.
